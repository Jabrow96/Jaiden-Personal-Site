Projects

Project 1 - Recreation of the Omnitrix From "Ben 10"

In October 2024, I took on the project of recreating the Omnitrix from my favorite childhood cartoon called Ben 10. I had recently gotten a 3D printer and wanted to try a bigger project than I had done before in school. I found a file of the watch online but I wanted MY watch to be able to retract and twist just like how it does in the show. So I ended up modifying the internals of the watch over the course of around three weeks and developed a successful mechanism after several prototypes. I then spent a couple hours priming, sanding, and painting it in order to get the esthetic I wanted.

Project 2 - Music Tempo Survey

This research project aimed to investigate the impact of music tempo on attention during a reaction-time task that simulates aspects of driving. Previous studies suggested that music can both help and hinder attention, depending on factors like tempo and task difficulty. By collecting data on reaction times and self-reported distraction, I hoped to better understand how music listening may influence safety-related behaviors. I created a survey to collect this data and linked it through a two different services in order to collect data from real people.
